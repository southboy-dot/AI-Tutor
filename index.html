About Juba Learn

Juba Learn is an educational platform designed specifically for the unique challenges faced by students in South Sudan. Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality education through a combination of AI-powered tutoring and connections with real teachers from the local community.

Education in South Sudan faces many challenges, including limited resources, teacher shortages, and infrastructure issues. Juba Learn aims to bridge these gaps by leveraging technology to deliver personalized learning experiences that adapt to each student's needs, pace, and learning style.

Our Vision

To create a world where every South Sudanese student has access to quality education regardless of their location, economic background, or connectivity limitations.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, engaging, and effective learning experiences that empower South Sudanese students to reach their full potential.

What Makes Us Different

  • Built specifically for the South Sudan curriculum and context
  • Designed to work with limited connectivity and on basic devices
  • Combines AI and human tutoring for the best of both worlds
  • Focuses on primary and secondary education in Math and Science
  • Created by a team with deep understanding of the region's educational needs

Our Services

AI Tutoring

Our AI tutor provides personalized learning assistance that adapts to each student's level and needs. Available 24/7, it can help with:

  • Explaining difficult concepts
  • Solving practice problems
  • Answering questions in real-time
  • Providing feedback on progress
  • Generating customized practice exercises

The AI tutor works even with intermittent internet connectivity, making it ideal for regions with limited access.

Real Teacher Connections

Connect with qualified local teachers from South Sudan when you need the human touch. Our network of educators can provide:

  • One-on-one tutoring sessions
  • Guidance on complex topics
  • Exam preparation support
  • Cultural context for learning concepts
  • Motivation and mentorship

Teachers are available based on scheduling and connectivity, providing a perfect complement to the always-available AI tutor.

Curriculum-Aligned Content

All learning materials are specifically designed to align with the South Sudan curriculum standards, ensuring relevance to national education goals.

  • Primary and secondary level content
  • Mathematics and Science focus
  • Culturally relevant examples and contexts
  • Progressive difficulty levels
  • Regular updates to match curriculum changes

Offline Learning Capabilities

Designed for the connectivity challenges of South Sudan, our platform offers robust offline capabilities:

  • Content caching for offline access
  • Low-bandwidth synchronization
  • Works on basic smartphones and tablets
  • Automatic resume when connectivity returns
  • Built for stability in challenging conditions
